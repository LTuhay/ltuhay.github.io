Saint Seiya Visual Novel

This visual novel (still in progress) was made using the Ren'Py engine, which is written in Python and utilizes the Pygame library.

During the game, inspired by the Twelve Temples saga of the Saint Seiya anime, you can enter your own name or any name you choose to play. Then, the characters or the narrator will present different options to progress.







Depending on the choices made earlier, new characters will appear, and different scenes will be unlocked. A wrong choice could lead to the end of the game. Be careful!
